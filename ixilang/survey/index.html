User Feedback on ixi lang

The ixi lang is a live coding system, attempting to address some issues identified in the live coding practice. Those shall not be described here, but in a paper that we're working on.

This survey on ixi lang will be instrumental for that paper. We hope that you can help us to analyse the ixi lang - and contribute to the field of live coding research - by answering this questionnaire. Obviously, both positive and negative views are enlightening and helpful.
