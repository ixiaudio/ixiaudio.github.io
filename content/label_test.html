label

The ixi label has been quiet. We've only released one CD, the "Same Same But Different" generative music project by Runar Magnusson and Thor Magnusson. A snapshot of that project can be heard at the rand%() radio.

Why the inactivity? Many reasons, one being that new technologies make pressing CDs and distributing them not as important as before. We are interested in grass-root music making where people already send mp3s and oggs to each other, bypassing the world of commercial music.

Another reason is that we're more interested in live music - alive music - either as generative music or improvisation and it is quite paradoxical to record and release such "music of the moment" experiences.
The third reason is time. We're quite busy doing other things and the label somehow is the one that drags behind. It might change soon.

We've had lots of people sending emails with links of music online made with ixi software. Thanks for that and please continue. We try to keep records of this and will contact you when we're releasing something, either on a CD or as a netlabel release.

If you are interested in sending in demos of your work, please contact infoixi-software.net.
