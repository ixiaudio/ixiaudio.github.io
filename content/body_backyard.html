The ixi backyard is a space where we throw some stuff that are not typical "ixi applications". I.e, software that directly explores the graphical interface as compositional or pattern generating catalyst. Here you can also find texts, tutorials, code snippets, workshop stuff and classes for SuperCollider, Python or PD.

Check the contents of the links here on the left and you can find quite a lot of stuff that we've developed and use in our workshops or our own work, typically things that relate to sensors, sensor interfaces, HI devices, OSC (Open Sound Control), Python, Java, Processing, SuperCollider, Pure Data, Max/MSP, etc.
