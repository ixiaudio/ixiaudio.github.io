Description

Slicer allows you to create rhythms and textures from any sound you like.
Import your sound and move the nodes. Slicer will chop your sound into 8 slices and superpose them. The slices will be created and manipulated acording to some variables that you can control with the control nodes (the ones represented by a square).
The grain nodes (in color) control volume and pan of each slice, while the control nodes control pitch, lenght, shift and starting point of the whole system.
