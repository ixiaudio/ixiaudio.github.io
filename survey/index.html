Survey on Musical Instruments

 

In the autumn of 2006 we conducted a phenomenological, qualitative survey on people's relationship with their acoustic and digitial instruments. This is part of an ongoing research. As for now [June, 2007] we have published one paper at NIME 2007 in New York on our findings, but there is more to come.
